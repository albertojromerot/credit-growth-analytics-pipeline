# Executive Summary Draft

## Responsible Credit Growth Through Next-Best-Action Analytics

This project demonstrates how a financial institution can prioritise customer credit opportunities using a responsible and commercially focused analytics pipeline.

Instead of ranking customers only by their likelihood to accept an offer, the model combines three dimensions:

1. likelihood of conversion;
2. likelihood of responsible credit behaviour;
3. expected economic value.

The result is an advisor-ready ranked list that supports better campaign targeting, more focused commercial execution, and stronger governance.

## Business Value

The solution is designed to help commercial and analytics teams:

- reduce manual segmentation effort;
- focus advisors on higher-value opportunities;
- avoid purely volume-driven credit targeting;
- compare rule-based segmentation against model-based ranking;
- monitor performance and governance through a dashboard layer.

## Outputs

The project produces:

- ranked customer recommendations;
- recommended product and action;
- reason codes;
- expected value estimates;
- model performance metrics;
- governance and data-quality checks;
- a treatment-log structure for future learning.

## Positioning

This is a project for finance, BI, customer analytics, credit risk analytics, and commercial data science scenarios.
