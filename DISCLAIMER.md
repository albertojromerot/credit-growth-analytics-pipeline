# Disclaimer

This repository is a public project for analytics, data science, and financial-services decision-support demonstration purposes.

## Synthetic data only

All datasets in this repository are synthetic. They do not represent real customers, real credit decisions, real financial products, real pricing, or any real institution's risk policy.

## Not credit advice or automated approval

The project demonstrates a responsible credit-growth Next-Best-Action pipeline. It is not intended to:

- approve or reject credit applications;
- replace underwriting, affordability, compliance, or risk-policy processes;
- provide financial, legal, regulatory, or credit advice;
- make automated decisions about real customers.

## Simplified modelling assumptions

The expected-value calculations, eligibility rules, responsible-lending filters, and synthetic labels are simplified for demonstration. Any real-world deployment would require independent validation, fairness assessment, privacy controls, data-protection review, compliance approval, monitoring, and human oversight.
